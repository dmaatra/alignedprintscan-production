Accessibility

Accessibility Notice

Aligned Print & Scan is committed to providing professional and accessible service experiences for clients seeking Remote Online Notary, mobile notary, and document support services.

Our Commitment

We aim to provide clear communication, readable website content, mobile-friendly access, and reasonable accommodations when possible.

Remote Online Notary Access

Clients participating in Remote Online Notary sessions should be able to communicate effectively during the session, understand the document being signed, and have access to a compatible camera-enabled device and stable internet connection.

Accommodation Requests

If language interpretation, accessibility assistance, or additional accommodations may be needed, please contact us in advance so we may coordinate appropriately whenever possible.
