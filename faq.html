Frequently Asked Questions

Answers about Remote Online Notary services, mobile appointments, witnesses, pricing, scheduling, cancellation, identification, and document support.

Remote Online Notary service allows an eligible notarization to be completed through a live audio-video session with a Texas commissioned online notary. The signer must complete the platform’s identity and technology requirements before the session can proceed.

The starting estimate includes a $25 online notarization service fee and one $10 notarial act. Each additional notarial act is estimated at $10. The final quote may also include approved document, witness, or other requested services.

A notarial act is each acknowledgment, jurat, oath, affirmation, or other authorized notarial certificate completed by the notary. The number of signers and the number of notarial acts are not always the same.

The online notary must be physically located in Texas. A signer may often be located elsewhere, subject to applicable law, document-recipient requirements, and the online notarization platform’s rules.

You need a camera-enabled device, stable internet, a supported browser, valid government-issued identification, access to email or text verification, and a quiet, well-lit environment.

A document witness signs the underlying document because the document requires a witness. A credible witness helps establish a signer’s identity when legally permitted. These roles are different and may require different screening and documentation.

Subject to availability, Aligned Print & Scan may coordinate a document witness. The current estimate is $25 per remote witness and $50 per mobile witness. Customer-provided witnesses are not charged a witness-coordination fee.

Appointments may take place at residences, offices, hospitals, care facilities, or another mutually agreed location where the signing can be completed safely and privately.
